Nasa-earthview

This website features a simple application for accessing images of the earth taken from earth's orbit by the Landsat 8 satellite. Images are taken approximately every 16 days. The website itself is built with AngularJS and Bootstrap and serves as an example of a simple-to-create and simple-to-use client-side web application. The displayed images are fetched from the NASA data portal.
